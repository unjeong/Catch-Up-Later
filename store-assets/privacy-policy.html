Catch Up Later

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Overview

Catch Up Later ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Key Point: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device and syncs only through your Chrome account.

Information We Collect

The Extension stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Website URLs: The URLs of websites you choose to monitor
  • Selectors: CSS selectors used to detect new posts
  • Post Data: Titles and links of detected posts for comparison
  • Settings: Your preferences such as check time and notification settings

How We Store Data

All data is stored using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • chrome.storage.sync: Small data (sites, settings) synced across your Chrome browsers via your Google account
  • chrome.storage.local: Larger data (post states) stored only on your current device

We do not operate any external servers. Your data never leaves your browser except through Chrome's own sync mechanism.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history beyond the sites you explicitly add
  • Login credentials
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Any data for advertising purposes

Permissions Explained

The Extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your monitored sites and settings
  • alarms: To schedule daily checks for new posts
  • activeTab: To detect posts on the current page when adding a site
  • scripting: To run content scripts that detect posts
  • notifications: To alert you when new posts are found
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): To fetch content from websites you choose to monitor

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

When fetching website content, we make direct requests to the websites you have added. These requests include standard browser headers but no personal information.

Data Deletion

You can delete all Extension data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" button in the Extension settings
  • Uninstalling the Extension from Chrome
  • Clearing Chrome's extension data in browser settings

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
